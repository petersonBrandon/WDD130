Overview

Purpose

To help people capture every adventure, birthday, holiday, event, etc. in a way that is more memorable and magical. To help people take better photos by imparting skills that I have learned over 10 years of photography.

Audience

Primary audience is youth and young adults ranging from ages 14 - 35. Secondary audience is adults above 35.
