Welcome to Brandon’s Photography Tips!

I have been a hobby photographer for about 10 years now and want to share what I have learned with anyone and everyone that wants to learn to take some wonderful photos! So I have created this website!

First, a little about me. I started photography when I was about 12/13 when my mom gave me one of her old cameras. I loved figuring out how it worked and seeing what I could do to take really pretty pictures. I would get done with school and homework and just go outside to take pictures of whatever I felt like. I personally really enjoy macro photography, taking pictures of really small things. However, over the years I have grow to really like landscape and portrait photography as well. I have had opportunities to photograph some beautiful places. From the jungles of Honduras to the deserts of Arizona, I have been to quite a few places and had the opportunity to photograph in many different conditions.

All of the pictures on this site, except the ones found on the gear page, are pictures that I personally have taken.
